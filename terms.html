1. Nature of the Agreement

This is a private, mutual, and non-legally-binding understanding between consenting adults, referred to in this framework as the Sugar Daddy and the Sugar Baby.

The purpose of the arrangement is to support a respectful, benefit-based companionship in which one party may offer time, communication, attention, and presence, while the other may provide financial support, gifts, or lifestyle assistance under mutually understood expectations.

This platform does not present itself as an employer, escort agency, or romantic contract provider, and nothing in this agreement should be interpreted as requiring unlawful conduct, emotional dependency, or guaranteed outcomes.

2. Physical Engagement Clause

If any arrangement includes in-person or physical interaction, both parties agree that all engagement must remain fully consensual, voluntary, non-exploitative, and respectful of personal boundaries at all times.

  • No party may use pressure, manipulation, intimidation, force, threats, or emotional coercion to obtain access, meetings, intimacy, or continued participation.
  • Every participant is expected to respect verbal and non-verbal boundaries immediately and without argument.
  • Any abusive, dangerous, or non-consensual behavior will be treated as a serious violation and may lead to permanent platform disqualification and referral to law enforcement where appropriate.

3. Virtual Interaction & Digital Conduct

Where an arrangement is online-only or digitally managed, both parties are expected to comply with applicable U.S. digital conduct standards and any relevant local laws. This includes lawful, respectful, and confidential communication.

  • No private or intimate content may be shared, reposted, leaked, saved for misuse, or distributed without explicit consent.

  • Messages, calls, and media exchanges must remain respectful, adult, and free from coercion or digital abuse.
  • Users understand that evidence of serious digital misconduct may be preserved for investigation and legal review.

4. Dispute Resolution & Legal Accountability

Serious breaches of trust, including fraud, non-payment, harassment, impersonation, identity misrepresentation, privacy violations, blackmail, or physical misconduct, may trigger internal investigation and external legal escalation.

  • Immediate suspension or permanent banning from the platform.
